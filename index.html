Welcome to Priyal Dance Studio &
Centre for Performing Arts

Rooted in the grace and rhythm of Indian Classical Dance,
especially Kathak, our studio is a space where passion, discipline, and creativity come together. Under the guidance of Smt. Priyal Najpande Gadkar,
we inspire dancers of all ages to explore the beauty of movement, the power of storytelling, and the essence of culture.
